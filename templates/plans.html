FAQ - Plans & Pricing

Do I get a 60 day free trial if I upgrade?

The 60 day free trial only applies if you register for a paying plan now. You can always downgrade later at any time.

What types of payment do you accept?

Currently, we accept PayPal and all major credit cards such as Visa, Mastercard, and American Express. At this time, we only take payments online, so we will not be able to invoice you, or take an order over the phone.

How does the 60 day free trial work?

All you need to do is simply enter your valid email address and create an username and password. After the initial 60 days, you will have an option to select a desired plan. Refunds will not be issued after the 60 day trial, and we cannot prorate for a partial month.
